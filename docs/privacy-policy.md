# Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 23, 2026

Finance Tracker helps users record transactions, review budgets, scan receipts, and ask AI questions about their own finances. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is used.

## Information The App Handles

- Account information: email address and authentication session data handled by Supabase Auth.
- Finance data: transactions, categories, dates, amounts, budgets, notes, and app preferences.
- Receipt data: receipt images selected or captured by the user, plus extracted merchant, total, line items, and category suggestions.
- AI prompts: questions or product comparison requests submitted to the AI assistant.
- Technical data: basic request logs from hosting, database, and monitoring services.

## How Information Is Used

The app uses this information to provide account access, save finance records, calculate dashboard insights, scan receipts, suggest budget guidance, and compare products when the user asks for market alternatives.

## Third-Party Services

Finance Tracker uses Supabase for authentication and database storage, Vercel for backend hosting, Google Gemini for AI features, and UptimeRobot for uptime monitoring. AI features may send relevant user-provided text, receipt images, or finance context to Gemini so it can produce a response.

## Data Sharing

Finance Tracker does not sell user data. Data is shared only with the services needed to run the app, process user-requested AI features, host the backend, or meet legal requirements.

## User Choices

Users can choose not to use receipt scanning or AI market comparison features. Users can export their transaction data as CSV from the app settings. Account and data deletion requests should be sent to the developer contact listed for the app.

## Security

Finance Tracker uses Supabase authentication and per-user database access controls. No security method is perfect, but the app is designed so users can only access their own finance records.

## Children

Finance Tracker is not intended for children under 13.

## Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact the app developer at the support email listed on the app store listing.
